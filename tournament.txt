Right now, the app does not have a good way for the organizer to contact the winner or collect payout details.

  What exists today:

  - Winner gets an in-app notification: “You won… organizer will pay out soon.”
  - Organizer sees a pending payout in the organizer dashboard.
  - Organizer sees only the winner user ID slice like Winner: abc12345...
  - Profiles have email in the database, but the app only shows email to the profile owner, not to organizers.
  - There is no chat system.
  - There are no bank / Telebirr / phone payout fields.

  So practically: today you cannot reliably contact/pay the winner from inside the app unless you already know them.

  What you should add is one of these:

  1. Simple payout contact fields
     Add fields to profile:
      - phone number
      - Telebirr number
      - bank name
      - bank account name
      - bank account number

     Then show those fields only to the tournament organizer after the player wins or has a pending payout.

  2. Payout request form
     When a player wins, show them a page: “Submit payout details.”
     They choose Telebirr or bank and submit details for that payout.
     Organizer sees the details in the payout dashboard, pays outside AddisPay, then clicks Mark paid.

  3. In-app messages
     Add chat between organizer and winner.
     This is more work than payout fields, and still does not solve structured bank details as cleanly.

  Best option for your app: add payout details to the winner payout flow, not full chat first.

  The clean flow would be:

  Tournament ends
  Winner gets notification
  Winner opens /me/payouts
  Winner enters Telebirr/bank details for that payout
  Organizer opens dashboard
  Organizer sees payout amount + winner payout details
  Organizer sends money manually
  Organizer clicks Mark paid
  Winner gets paid notification

  That is the missing piece in your current system.
